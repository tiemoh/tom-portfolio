Competitor Targeting Platform

A concept for a US market intelligence platform that connects workflow setup and dashboard analysis. Users can define attack and defend locations, build weighted metrics, and score opportunity around brand and competitor locations.

  • Metric builder using selectable attributes and adjustable weighting
  • Brand and competitor POI filtering with walk or drive catchments
  • US-specific demand, pressure, and whitespace opportunity scoring
Open Work Example Portfolio Repo
UI Outline First work item

How the interface would work

Workflow panel

Brand, audience, catchment type, travel mode, drive-time, and geography selection sit in a left-side configuration rail.

Metric builder

Analysts choose attributes from demographics, expenditure, and POI layers, then apply weightings to create reusable market metrics.

Map and catchments

The central canvas shows brand locations, competitor locations, and isoline-based walk or drive catchments with scored opportunity overlays.

Insight output

A right-side panel ranks attack and defend opportunities and explains which inputs are driving each score.

Example metric: Market Demand = population + local spend + food spend, combined into a weighted grocery potential score.
